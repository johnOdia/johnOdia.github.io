I studied Business Administration in the university of Benin where i graduated with a BSc in Business Administration. I learnt a lot from my course of study at the university. Primarily i learnt how to structure organizations into working smaller units and set goals for each. Also, i learnt that leadership is perhaps the most important skill that a manager needs to develop in order to be able to motivate surbordinates towards goal achievement. This skill has enabled me in my everyday life to work in teams.

Finally, i fell in love with coding and building of applications during my service year in 2019 and i started with the basic web development course on sololearn where i earned certificates in html css and javascript. I also used resources like freecodecamp and codecademy to learn and improve my skills. The reason i went into coding was so that i will be very versatile in app creation. I belief that my education as a manager and my training as a software engineer will enable me to get to the apex of the software engineering profession.
